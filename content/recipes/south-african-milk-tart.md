## Ingredients
* 2 cups milk (2% or full cream)
* 2 tablespoons butter
* 1/2 cup sugar
* Pinch of salt
* 3 eggs
* 3 tablespoons cornstarch, heaped
* Pinch of cinnamon sugar
* Pinch of coconut
* 1 graham cracker pie shell

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350F.
1. Rinse pot with cold water. Add butter, sugar and pinch of salt to milk. Bring milk to a boil, turn to low.
1. Beat eggs very well in a separate container. Have at the ready.
1. Combine cornstarch with a little extra milk to dissolve and have at the ready.
1. Once milk is boiling, add maizena mixture in a slow stream, stirring until it resembles a thick porridge (not too thick). Add well beaten eggs in a slow stream and whisk briskly, until all melded together.
1. Pour mixture into pie shell and immediately sprinkle with cinnamon sugar. Bake 15-20 mins. Sprinkle with coconut once it comes out of the oven.
