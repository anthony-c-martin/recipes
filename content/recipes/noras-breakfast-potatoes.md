## Ingredients
* Potatoes
* Olive oil
* Cumin
* Red pepper flakes
* McCormicks roasted garlic seasoning mix

## Instructions
1. Dice potatoes into 1" cubes
1. Toss potatoes with oil & spices, season with salt & pepper.
1. Bake at 425F for around 30-40 minutes, stirring halfway through.
